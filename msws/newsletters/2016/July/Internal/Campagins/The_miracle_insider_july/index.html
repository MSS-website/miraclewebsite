WUG User group meeting
July 13th, 2016
Being a member of IBM Middleware User Community (IMWUC), Miracle in collaboration with IBM will be on July 13th, 2016 at Miracle HQ organising an IMWUC user group event. It provides a sneak peak into the most recent updates to traditional and liberty runtime WebSphere options. Apart from helping to view the vast variety of options on IBM Bluemix with WAS for Java workloads, the event also covers Enterprise Connectivity, Database Options and Entry Points for WAS on Cloud along with a Live Demo.
