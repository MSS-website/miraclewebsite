Sports Gala '15
December 12, 2015
"Win if you can, Lose if you must, But never quit" was the main motto for the players who have participated in the Miracle Annual Sports Gala which was held on December 12th, 2015. As a part of sports gala all the team games like Volley Ball and Throw Ball were organised along with Shuttle, Table Tennis, Chess and Caroms. All the players participated with zeal and passion, and that made the crowd encourage their respective teams around.
 
 
Musical Night '15
December 16, 2015
Is music your favourite zone?? Obviously everyone says yes. In the beautiful night the moon stared, the stars dazzled with smile, chilling fog played horn for Miraclites at the amazing musical night of Miracle. Kiran Mamidi, Swaroop Pusapati, Satish Mongam, Ajay Teddi and many other employees came together and showcased their musical talent with Guitars, Drums and songs and have stolen the hearts of everyone at the event. And the day ended with the memories that enlightened the hearts of our employees.

Christmas
December 25, 2015
"Star of wonder, star of night, star of royal, beauty bright." The star is the one which represents the birth of Jesus Christ which is celebrated as his birthday on December 25th.
