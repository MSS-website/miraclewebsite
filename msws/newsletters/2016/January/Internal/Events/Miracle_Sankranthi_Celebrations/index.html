Miracle Sankranthi Celebrations
Spring is on its way - come fly a kite!
 
Flame your past in Bhogi, invite new hopes with Sankranthi and enjoy all the tastes with Kanuma. It is the start of the festivals, and the harvest festival is here. Being celebrated as an honor to Lord Indra, the God of Clouds and Rains, special prayers are offered for having an abundant harvest. With colorful rangolis, bonfires, with people preparing savories and sweets it is in fact the most enjoyable period where all assemble and celebrate.
 
With colorful kites flying all over the sky and crops ready to be harvested, the four day festival indeed has a very special place in Indian culture. To celebrate the Harvest festival, kites flying is going to be organised at M-City and M-Heights, on January 15th, 2016 from 12PM to 2PM IST at M-City and 3PM to 4PM IST at M-Heights along with bonfire at M-City apartments and cafeteria on January 14th, 2016 at 4 PM IST. All employees are invited to have fun at this event on Cheer the smiles and come together to celebrate this festival with great zeal and pomp.
