Unity Walk

With a view to spread a message about ‘clean and green IT city’ among the residents of Visakhapatnam during the ongoing generation, Team Miracle organized a 'Unity walk' at the Victory at Sea(RK Beach) and walked along the roads of RK Beach on December 14th,2014. And on 13th December 2014 Miracle Software Systems CEO Mr.Prasad Lokam along with his team released a press note regarding the “Unity Walk” and as a result the highlights of the unity walk were published on the next day in some of the local newspapers. Flagging off the “Unity Walk” by our beloved CEO, more than 150 employees participated in the walk by holding Cloth Banners and Helium balloons to promote vizag as a clean and green IT hub. Despite the event being on a weekend many of them took part in the unity walk which lead to a great success.

Medical Camp

A free Medical Camp in some villages adjacent to Bhogapuram marked the 20 years celebrations of Miracle Software Systems that took place on 18th and 19th december 2014. The Medical camp on 18th December 2014 was set up in Munjeru and Kongavani Palem villages that are nearby Miracle city and on 19th December,2014 the free medical camp was set up in Petapeta and Rama Chandra Peta where almost 1000 villagers gathered over the 2 days camp. All the four doctors who came all over from NRI hospital visakhapatnam coordinated with the volunteers from Miracle Software Systems and organised the free medical camp where they provided medicines to the village localities along with the doctors prescription which made it a big success.
