DataPower - SOMA Scripts for
Auto-Deployment and What Next?
On-Demand Webinar
Are you an Enterprise with a growing number of Datapower Appliances? With security being at the forefront of all Global Organizations, it has become a huge challenge to progress the change between environments and appliances. The present approach of deployment through Manual Exports (or) Configuration of the objects via Web GUI is inherently error-prone and introduces inconsistencies across appliances. This creates a need to move towards a more automated, consistent and proven deployment mechanism.
In this On-Demand Webinar hear from our experts how you can automate your DataPower deployments, Export configurations and reuse them across multiple environments. Our team will also demonstrate some of the deployment options using the XML Management Interface that can be promoted using Automated Deployment Techniques.
