Miracle Employee Premier League 2015
We are back the Miracle's Annual Cricketing Event where the best of the best are set to clash their weapons in battle
 
Player Registration
 
Cricket is back to Miracle, with the Miracle's Annual Employee Premier League all set to hit the battle ground this August. We saw the mighty Miracle City Development Team take the precious trophy home after a grueling battle with our very own SAP Development Team. This year we are back again to give these teams a chance to prove themselves.
 
To spice up the challenge we are introducing a new Franchise Model, which allows 2 Players to fund a franchise and buy players of their choice at an Auction. The registration for a franchise will be 2000 INR and needs to be payed before August 6th, 2015. For more information regarding registering your franchise please contact Janaki Ram Bammidi (8985064953) (or) Chinni Venkata Aditya (9701002728).
 
Important : Franchises will need to pay the registration, register their team name and logo, and also procure their own dress (mandatory). Auctions for the tourney will be held this week along with an optional tour to Dazzle (Official TShirt Vendor for Miracle Sports Teams for the last 6 years). Each team will be given a 3000 INR Bank within which they will need to buy players.
 
Hurry up as we will only accept the first 8 franchises for the tourney. With over 30 matches to be played before deciding the champion, this is set to be one of the grandest and largest EPL's in Miracle History.
 
Are you a player interested in participating in the auction? If you are, then the first step to your participation is to register yourself right here. Players who fail to register before August 7th, 2015 will not be considered during the auction rounds.
