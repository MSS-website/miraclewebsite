Miracle Employee Premier League 2015

Player Registration
 
Miraclites,
 
We hope you are ready because we are ready for some action. The EPL is all but rage at Miracle this year, and we have more than 180 player registrations already over the last 2 days with the franchise spots also filling up rapidly.
 
If you haven't registered yourself yet for the auction register here today.
 
If you are a franchise and are looking to book your spot hurry up and meet a coordinator today without fail. You can reach us at 9701002728 (Aditya), 90001110011 (Janaki Ram) and 9581018585 (Varma). Teams which have payed the registration fees on (or) before August 7th, 2015 will be considered for the tourney.
 
The auction for the franchises and players will take place at Lawsons Bay Colony(Miracle Office) from 01:00 PM IST to 05:00 PM IST. Franchises failing to turn up for the auction on time will be disqualified and only 50% of the registration will be refunded.
 
Auction Rules:
  1. The Franchise Owner must join the team without participating in the auction by using 200 INR in his bank
  2. The Franchise can have one Idol Player stand for their team without participating in the auction by using 300 INR in his bank
  3. A Franchise must purchase a minimum of 13 players and a maximum of 16 players
  4. Only the Franchise Owner and Franchise Idol Player are allowed to be present at the auction; No visitors, replacements (or) audience are allowed
  5. Video of the auction will be recorded and distributed after the auction
  6. The base price for a player is 50 INR, from where the bidding will begin
  7. A Total of 2 Rounds will be held for the auction; A Player who fails to be bought for both rounds will be left out of the tourney without a team
  8. A bank of 3000 INR virtual money will be given to each franchise for the auction; They must buy all their players within that amount
  9. The maximum bidding price for a player is 1500 INR; In case of a tie between two teams at the end of 2 rounds of bidding for the player, the team with the highest remaining back-base amount required for minimum players will be given the player
  10. The minimum bank required for a team is (Number of players required to become a 13 squad*50 INR)
  11. Any team which bids more than the money they have will be fined 50 INR from their virtual bank
  12. Any sort of manipulation (or) mischief during the auction will lead to a fine of 200 INR for the Franchise
  13. All Fines will be deducted from the bank of the franchise
  14. A Sample Round of 10 players will be auctioned off at the start of the auction as practice for all Franchises
  15. The Auction Coordinating Committee will have final say in all decisions and has the authority to reverse (or) confirm a decision
  16. A reading and explanation of all rules will take place during the auction
