Missing Tail
July 31, 2015
Missing tail is a fun event which was organized among our employees on 31st July 2015 at Miracle City and Miracle Heights. Each participant was blindfolded and was challenged to pin a tail to the picture of a horse with a missing tail. All the participants were given chocolates and the winners were presented keychains engraved with Miracle logo. The event got a huge response as Miraclites enjoyed the joyful and amusing event.
