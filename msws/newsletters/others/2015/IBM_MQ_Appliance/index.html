Introducing IBM MQ Appliance

 A new way to deploy MQ Messaging
For more than 20 years, IBM MQ has been providing thousands of customers with mission-critical messaging, connecting applications, systems, and services with reliability and security. IBM continues to invest in its enterprise messaging strategy and products to suit new business needs.

As companies expand their application deployments or become more geographically distributed, the number of connected applications will grow, increasing the need for more and more messaging to ensure connectivity remains simple, reliable and manageable. As these messaging deployments roll out, organizations are also under pressure to ensure that maintaining and operating a complex messaging infrastructure does not see an increase in costs along with increased messaging deployments. To help ensure that the operation and maintenance of a MQ network is simplified while reducing costs and complexity, IBM has announced a new state-of-the-art messaging appliance.

IBM MQ Appliance provides customers with a new way to simplify and speed up the deployment and maintenance of your enterprise messaging implementations, providing you flexibility to scale throughput to meet this changing environment while continuing to offer the reliability and security you expect from IBM MQ wherever you choose to deploy the IBM MQ Appliance. This appliance builds on IBM’s long time leadership in the enterprise messaging space with IBM MQ and leverages on our strong position in appliances through IBM DataPower.

Simply, IBM MQ Appliance provides the scalability and security of IBM MQ v8 with the convenience, rapid deployment and lower total cost of ownership of an appliance. IBM MQ Appliance will allow customers to consolidate multiple queue managers within a single physical appliance to reduce datacenter complexity and day-to-day operational tasks with simpler management and maintenance.

With IBM MQ Appliance, customers can gain outstanding advantages, a selection of these are:
  • Rapid time to deploy, easy to configure and run for both enterprise and remote    deployments
  • Simple out of the box High-Availability with paired connectivity to another appliance
  • Easy maintenance through certified firmware updates
  • Reduce total cost of ownership while reducing overall maintenance and administration    costs
  • Two models that offer price points to match different user cases
  • Browser-based user interface, IBM MQ Console, for monitoring and administration of    MQ resources
  • High levels of performance and throughput with MQ implementation optimized for    appliance hardware out-of-the-box
