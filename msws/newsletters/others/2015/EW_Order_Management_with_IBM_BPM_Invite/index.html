With an ever-evolving and demanding market, enterprises are looking at options to reinvent their internal and external processes to ensure that they are flexible for rapid changes. Experience increased business agility, productivity and collaboration through standards based integration to disparate Operational Support Systems (OSS) / Business Support Systems (BSS), including heritage applications. In this webinar you will learn how to reduce operating expenses through standardized, streamlined and flexible end-to-end processes.
Join us on August 4th, 2015(Tuesday) at 11:00 AM EST and hear from our experts on how you can streamline your order management processes with a consistent and common process spanning for new and existing customers, multiple product lines and channels, business partners, suppliers and Lines of Businesses. Reliability, Automation and Security are absolutely critical for an enterprise’s successful B2B operations.
