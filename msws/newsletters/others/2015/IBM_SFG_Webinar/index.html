Why IBM SFG is your Best Choice when Migrating
from C:E for UNIX : Roadmap Session
Register Here
For many enterprises, a popular business integration method for their partner community is the exchange of files. Being a straightforward way to exchange information between systems and partners, files offer flexibility between heterogeneous enterprises and infrastructures. Although file transfer seems simple enough, large enterprises need to be able to audit these data exchanges while also requiring reliability, security and cross-platform connectivity.
Connect : Enterprise for UNIX was a great solution for your Managed File Transfer needs, but the time is now to move on as IBM sunsets the support of the product. Join us on August 5th, 2015 (Wednesday) at 2:30 PM EST, as MFT experts from Miracle talk about IBM Sterling File Gateway which not only provides a alternative to IBM C:E, it provides a much better and brighter future as well. The session will also cover guidance on End-of-Service/End-of-Life for managing your budget for the migration. If you have questions around the migration and next steps for your C:E migration this would be a great session for you to attend and get the migration cycle started.
