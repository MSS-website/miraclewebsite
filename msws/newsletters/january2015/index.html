Inside Miracle - January 2015
Fight for a Cause
Every year we celebrate India's greatest victory as our very well known Republic Day, which was when our constitution was formed 66 years ago. To honor and commemorate the heroics and efforts of our patriots on this great day Team Miracle celebrated its success with the local children. The teams at Miracle Heights helped a school to get back on their feet by donating stationery, giving moral support and guidance and also helped to build two new water tanks for the school as well..
 
On this grand occasion the Miracle City teams also rallied together to help and donated water purifiers, wall clocks, stationery, chocolates, flags, first-aid kits and plants to 6 schools in and around Miracle City. More than 60 volunteers came forward and we were able to give new hope to the children to come up in their future with flying colours. Their voices echoed through the day as they celebrated India's victory, and head home with new hope and motivation.

Pongal Celebrations
Pongal is considered as the harvest festival of South India. It is one of the most important and popular Hindu festivals that we celebrated from January 14th, 2015 to January 16th, 2015. On the first day known as Bhogi, all the employees of Miracle city lighted up a big camp fire early in the morning at Miracle City Apartments and later in the morning all the labour working at Miracle were given traditional clothes.

 
At Miracle heights we had a Traditional Attire competition where all the employees took an active part and came forward with different traditional costumes. Lakshmi Priyanka Surampudi and Durga Prasad Pyla were awarded for the best traditional attire competition. The second day was Perum Pongal, the most important festival which is also called as Surya Pongal. It is celebrated to pay respects to Surya, the Sun God. On this occasion Team Miracle had a traditional dining at Miracle Heights with huge varieties of recipes.

Pedaling on the Miracle roads
As an employee in Miracle, there is a high probability that we wouldn't have pedaled for years and if so, only in the Gym. And this makes way for yet another reason for your stay in Miracle city campus to turn even more exciting. Cycles are a traditional way of commutation in the Miracle city campus. Cycling gives you a great sense of pleasure and helps to enable quicker movement to different buildings inside the campus without causing pollution and to sustain a friendly environment atmosphere.
 
Cycling in the cool breeze at midnight will be as much fun as it was in doing the same on the Miracle city campus roads. There is a considerable percentage of people who do not know how to ride a bicycle and only learnt after coming to Miracle campus.If you get late enjoying your favorite food in the evening, grabbing a cycle would help you get to the farthest of food courts in a few minutes. If the pleasure of cycling seems an over hyped affair to you, then let it be. The matter of fact is that even small things like these later go on to become a part of really happy memories.
