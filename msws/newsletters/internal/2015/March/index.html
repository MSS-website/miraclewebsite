Celebration Of Colors
On the eve of Holi, the employees of Miracle had a splendid day in Miracle City and Miracle Heights on 6th of March 2015 as all our employees splashed up with different coloured costumes resembling bright stars for an amazing evening. Holi, the only festival of colours is inspired by the original Indian Holi fest, a major festival in Hindu culture which is celebrated on the day after the last full moon day of the Hindu month of Phalguna which falls between late February and early March in western calendars. It resembles the cheerful transition from dark-harsh winter to bright-warm spring. People dress up in white and come together to share dance, music, visual stimulation and performance art.
 
People cast on others bright colored powders and scented water as the barriers between children and elderly, rich and poor, man and woman and friend and stranger are removed. On the previous day of Holi, our employees were informed to come to work in different coloured costumes. And, the team that was dazzled in colours of Rainbow was announced and was gifted with a beautiful Glass Memento labelled"Kaleidoscopic Team" with a beautiful quote on it. Finally, everyone got drenched up playing with colours as all of us made our day colourful and joyful with different costumes.

AU Mega Job Drive
Millennium Software Solutions and AU have partnered in hosting a 2 day job fair with around 14 companies including Miracle Software Systems on 7th and 8th of March 2015 at Andhra University College of Engineering in Visakhapatnam. Around 3,500+ job seekers have appeared for our interviews with 2,320 accounting for software trainee positions and the rest for open positions in IT-Sales, US-IT Recruitment and Marketing departments. AP HRD Minister Mr. Ganta Srinivasa Rao and AU Vice Chancellor Prof. G.S.N. Raju have headed the offer letters giving ceremony on Day 1 at YVS Murthy Auditorium of AU.
 
On Day 2, the job offers ceremony started with the opening speech of AU registrar Mr. V Uma Maheswara Rao welcoming our CEO Mr. Prasad Lokam, CEO of Millenium Software Solutions Mr. Sridhar Reddy, CEO's of 2 other companies and AU NCC in-charge to lead the event. He also congratulated all the selected people and specially congratulated all the selected women as the day 8th March is International Women's Day. Our CEO Mr. Prasad Lokam took the opportunity to share about Miracle's Vision of having 50,000 smart employees by year 2020. Around 300+ job seekers were issued spot job offers from Miracle and various news dailies, TV channels, websites from in and around the state have published news about this mega drive.
