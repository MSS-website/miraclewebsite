Our Partnerships with Axway and Atlassian
Continuing our growth pattern, we are now partnered with two software giants to establish ourselves better in the B2Bi/EDI space with Axway and the APM space with Atlassian. These partnerships will enable us to become more competent in the Application Lifecycle Management and B2B Integration spaces.

 
 
Miracle Enablement Webinar Series
Internal Communications and Knowledge Sharing is very important. Last month we have started a new Webinar series for internal employees for them to gain more knowledge about the industry, internal policies and the overall company. These webinars will be displayed live and soon will also be available as replays .
Webinar dates and registrations will be found on the Internal Webinar Depot page which is set to be released in mid-July.
ICE Team Formation
Integration, Connectivity and Messaging have always been synonymous with Miracle, and taking this into consideration we have recently started a new team called ICE (Integration Center of Excellence) . This team will be focussed on using technology to innovate for new integration trends for Future Digital Enterprises.
We expect to see a big bang of news from them in the upcoming months with some innovative topics and demos.
