Become a writer
Translation of human understanding into artful literary presentation is named as the skill of writing. Everybody dreams of becoming a great writer. However facts reveal that not everyone masters the skills of writing. Perfect writing doesn't start from day 1 but it gets better with practice. Some of the basic necessities of a well written content are, excellent vocabulary, useful information and proper sentence formation. Building up your vocabulary lets you have command over words. Various activities for acquiring knowledge can include reading articles, newspapers, watching videos, participating in debates, attending conferences, making discussions, listening to podcasts, reading blogs, forums, social networking and browsing the internet. Keep track of your thoughts and knowledge by maintaining a separate book for it. Before you start writing, there are some essential tasks that you as an aspiring writer need to have in your writing skills development plan.

Figure out what you want to write, read and learn more about the topic, share views with others who are familiar with the topic. Come up with a list of short and effective sentences/words which attract your readers, put yourself in yours readers shoes and fantasize the situation. And, set a routine on when and where you have to write, move to surroundings which inspire you to write and make yourself comfortable. While writing use simple words in the beginning and do not deviate from the topic. Remember quotes and try to interpret photos and doodles. After you are done writing ask reviews from community and know different opinions. Make sure to proofread the content you write. Do it slowly and do it twice. The ultimate job of a prospective writer is to make sure that readers receive exact message. The prospective writer should not only have a clear idea about the current article but also have an idea of which articles have to be written in future.
