Our Valentines
Crush refers to an emotion of a strong attraction. Crush is not only a wonderful feeling but also someone who is very special to you. Every time you see that special person your heart beats and you try to hide a smile. Having a crush on someone is imagining yourself being with the person, but never actually being with. All the Miraclites celebrated an event on 13th February, 2015 on the eve of Valentine’s Day naming it as “Vote For Your Crush”. And two male and female employees who got the highest number of votes were declared as “Mr. Valentine” and “Ms. Valentine”.
 
As evening came through the crowds gathered anxiously to see who was crowned as Miracle’s valentine and within seconds the word got out that names of Mr. Valentine and Ms. Valentine were being announced by Mr. Swaroop Varma of the HR department. Mr. Prasad (IIB) & Ms. Annapurna (EAI) from Miracle City and Mr. Surendra Raju (SAP) & Ms. Kusumanjali (Family Dollar) from Miracle Heights were declared as Mr. Valentine and Ms. Valentine respectively.
