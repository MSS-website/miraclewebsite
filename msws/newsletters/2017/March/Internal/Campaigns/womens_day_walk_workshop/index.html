Celebrating Women
Dedicate to Women everywhere
Showcase The Girl Power!
 
International Women's Day is celebrated for the recognition of women for their achievements in IT, Social, Sports and many other sectors. To celebrate this day, our team is conducting a unity march for the women in IT (Employees and Students) from YMCA to RK Beach, Visakhapatnam on March 12th, 2017 at 07:00 AM IST to showcase their social integrity and strength.
Nowadays IT world is developing with a high pace and leaving its mark everywhere. Miracle team is conducting a workshop on IoT to create awareness on latest technologies for the women in colleges on March 11th, 2017 at 10:00 AM IST at Miracle Heights, Rushikonda. This will help them in exploring the IT world and experiencing the latest tools.
