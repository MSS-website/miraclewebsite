Announcing SHA-1 Collision
SHA-1 Cryptographic Algorithm used for hashing files had collision of hashes and is declared as unsafe to use in any kind of business transactions and web based activity. A period of 90 days is given by Google to disclose their method and the two PDF’s which caused the SHA-1 Collision. As a remission, we need to switch from SHA-1 to SHA-256 and SHA-3 for our future developments and websites having certificates signed by SHA-1 will be marked as “Neutral” and “Lacking of Security” irrespective of HTTP or HTTPS.

PROLOG
Prolog is a logic programming language correlated with Artificial Intelligence techniques used for theorem proving, type inference systems, and automated planning. It is expressed in terms of relations and is used by NASA to build a software named Clarissa for the ISS and is well suited for specific tasks like searching databases, voice control systems, and filling templates.
